Analysis

Based on the graphs above, it appears that assault calls are consistent in Fairfax City throughout the year. Assault calls appear to be higher in the summer and fall (May-October). The graph also displays a few days where assaults did not occur. February 2nd, September 4th and 30th, and October 26th have days where no assaults took place. The cold spots appears to be January-March which has fewer assault calls since 2007. I predict that there will be around 20 assault calls in August of 2023. Based on the graph, August continues to had a moderate level of assault calls.

Based on the hour of day graph, assault calls occur more frequently during evening and night. The hotspot for assault calls occur between 2:00 PM-12:00 AM. Some days will have a higher number of assaults but in general they will increase in the evening. In regards to coldspots, assault calls occur less between 3:00 AM-7:00AM. 6:00 AM will have the lowest number with little to no assault call everyday throughout the week. Based on the graph, the pattern for assault calls will occur more later in the night around 10:00 PM and will decrease during the morning and rise again in the afternoon.

From the day of week graph, it appears assault calls are place everyday. However, on the weekends there is more activity. There more assault calls on Friday-Sunday. For coldspots, there is less activity on Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Based on this data, I expect there is be more assault calls Friday and Saturday and fewer calls on Tuesday. Because most people are off work on the weekends and people attend social gatherings, I predict there will be more assaults.
